About Maharshi Barium Salts

Maharshi Barium Salts is a chemical manufacturing enterprise specializing in the production of high-purity Barium Carbonate and Sodium Sulphide. Our products serve industries including ceramics, glass, pesticides, textiles, and pharmaceuticals.

With a strong focus on quality, safety, and consistency, we follow strict manufacturing standards to ensure reliable supply and customer satisfaction.

Located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India, we are committed to long-term partnerships and ethical industrial practices.
